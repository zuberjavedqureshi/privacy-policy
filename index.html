Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Text Toolkit collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Text Toolkit is a utility app that helps users repeat, transform, clean, and share text. We value your privacy and aim to keep the app simple and respectful of your data.

1. Information We Collect

Text Toolkit does not require you to create an account or sign in.

The app may store limited information locally on your device to support app features, such as:

  • saved favorites
  • recent repeater outputs
  • tool settings and preferences
  • theme and app behavior preferences

This information is stored on your device and is not uploaded by the app to our own servers.

2. Text You Enter

Text you type or paste into Text Toolkit is processed locally on your device for app functionality.

Your text is only shared outside the app when you explicitly choose actions such as:

  • copying content
  • sharing content with another app
  • exporting content to a file

3. Advertising

The free version of Text Toolkit may display banner advertisements using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect certain device and usage information in accordance with Google’s own policies. This may include identifiers, diagnostic data, and ad interaction data used to deliver and measure ads.

Please review Google’s privacy policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-App Purchases

Text Toolkit may offer a Pro version through in-app purchases. Purchase transactions are handled by the platform provider, such as Google Play, and not directly by Text Toolkit.

We do not collect or store your payment card or billing details ourselves.

5. Data Storage and Retention

App data such as favorites, local history, and settings may be stored on your device using local storage technologies provided by the platform.

This locally stored data generally remains on your device until you clear it, reset the app, or uninstall the application, subject to your device and platform behavior.

Text Toolkit does not maintain your entered text, favorites, or personal content on our own servers.

6. Third-Party Services

Text Toolkit may rely on third-party services for features such as advertising, billing, and platform functionality. These services may operate under their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the app. Because most app data is stored locally on your device, protection of that information also depends on your device security, software updates, and access controls.

8. Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, investigate fraud, or comply with legal obligations.

9. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection and use of app-related information by uninstalling Text Toolkit using the standard uninstall process available on your device or app marketplace.

10. Children’s Privacy

Text Toolkit is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the concern.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
